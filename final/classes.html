pre-calculus

dr. kaminsky

in pre-calculus so far, we've reviewed algebra 2, learned conic sections, trigonometric functions, the unit circle, trigonometric identities and algebraic proofs. we've had two projects so far: financial and trigonometric modeling, and we have another that we're currently doing as the final. i like math as i can rely on it to always present a logical solution. everything works, and everything just makes sense. i think it's one of the most unique subjects, as it requires a very unique style of thinking that utilizies a combination of logic, problem solving, visualiziation, and creative and critical thinking.

in scientific thinking 3 so far, we've learned about lab safety, states of matter, and astronomy, and lots more - we've actually touched on a very diverse range of topics. i like science because it really gets into how and why the world works, which i think is so fascinating, especially since the concepts we learn can be applied to everyday life. for example, we've recently learned about color and and how it is perceived. i find this interesting as color is a very applicable thing in our lives because we literally see it all the time!
