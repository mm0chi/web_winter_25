a brief history lesson

studio ghibli is a japanese animation film studio founded in 1985 by directors miyazaki hayao and isao takahata, as well as producer toshio suzuki. it is well known for its high quality animation, vivid storytelling, vibrant artistry, and unique/cutesy characters. studio ghibli is also well regardered for its timeless classics which you may have heard of: my neighbor totoro, howl's moving castle, spirited away, kiki's delivery service - the list goes on! i will be reviewing some (see reviews), so hopefully that inspires you to explore the comfort + coziness of the world of ghibli

why ghibli?

i recommend studio ghibli because of its high quality animation and use of artistry to effectively tell stories. because of its resonant themes of love, family, and self-discovery. but also because it genuinely has such a cozy, peaceful, and almost familiar ambience to it. you also find a lot of comfort in the characters, too - with their unique quirks and cute designs (i love totoro, gigi, and no face). overall, watching a ghibli film is like hugging your mother or drinking a warm cup of tea, so i'd definitely recommend!
